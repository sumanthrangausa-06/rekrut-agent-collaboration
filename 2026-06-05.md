- **Failure pattern:** Multi-file modifications (e.g., "fix all TypeScript errors") always fail
- **Solution:** Micro-task principle — break into single-file, single-component tasks
- **Strategy:** Spawn 5 agents in parallel for 5 files instead of 1 agent for 5 files
